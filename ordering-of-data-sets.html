Chapter 6 Ordering of data sets

Data can be ordered by size or alphabetically (ascending or descending). sort(), rank() und order().

With sort() the values are sorted by size, default is ascending. rank() specifies the ranks of the values in the ordered order. order() has the practically reversed function, i. the first element is the smallest value of the component x, etc.

#Ordering of data

x=c(4,6,2,7,9,1,5,4)
x
## [1] 4 6 2 7 9 1 5 4
sort(x)
## [1] 1 2 4 4 5 6 7 9
x
## [1] 4 6 2 7 9 1 5 4
sort(x,decreasing=T)
## [1] 9 7 6 5 4 4 2 1
rank(x)
## [1] 3.5 6.0 2.0 7.0 8.0 1.0 5.0 3.5
order(x)
## [1] 6 3 1 8 7 2 4 5
