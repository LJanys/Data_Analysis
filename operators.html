Chapter 3 Operators

The basic arithmetic operations are

+, -, *, /

Decimals are declared with a “.”, not a comma

1.8 not 1,8

Exponents are declared with ^, i.e. $2^3 $ is written as

2^3
## [1] 8

round(...,x)  rounding to x decimals.
